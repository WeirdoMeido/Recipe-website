Fierabrás Balsam

The inedible version!

Legends say that Sir Don Quijote de la Mancha once told Sancho Panza the recipe of this unrivaled beverage, which some say, is able to heal any wound, no matter how mortal.

Boil the following ingredients in any cauldron you may have lying around ya' house:

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Wine
  • Salt
  • Rosemary

Procedure

  1. Throw the ingredients listed above (add more if you want!)
  2. Boil at 100°C for a day (Don't look at me, I didn't make this up!)
  3. Cool down for 3 days
  4. Serve in a crystal cup, and enjoy! (If you want to meet in the afterlife, that is...)
